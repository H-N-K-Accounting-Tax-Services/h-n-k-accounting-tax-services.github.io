Services

Tax

Competent tax preparation and planning can help you to minimize your future tax liability. We can help you manage both your personal and your business tax issues and understand how upcoming business opportunities impact your tax status. By having us on your team, you are more likely to benefit from opportunities that may not be readily apparent. We understand how the latest federal, state, and local tax legislation and other developments affect you and your business and are constantly identifying new ways to reduce those tax liabilities.

Payroll

Payroll management are an ongoing challenge for business owners because of constantly changing federal, state and local laws and tax regulations. We are professionals well versed on these laws and regulations, and can proactively alert you to material changes that will affect your business and your employees while also keeping payroll running smoothly, no matter how large your business and your employee population become.

Advisory

As a true business partner, we are available to help you handle any business problem or opportunity. We are ready to engage in business consulting projects to help you make the right decisions for your business's future. If you face questions related to expanding, selling or restructuring your business, we have the necessary business acumen and analytical capabilities to help you make the correct choices.

Notary Public

A notary public can help you to handle various business matters, including witnessing and authenticating document execution and signatures, such as housing deeds and wills. We offer Notary Public services, and are available when you need us.

Bookkeeping

If your business does not currently have accounting staff, or if your business is outgrowing its current bookkeeping system, we can provide comprehensive bookkeeping services to ensure the smooth operation of your business's finances. As your business needs grow and change, we can provide planning assistance to make sure your financial and accounting systems keep pace with your business needs.

Financial Statements

Financial statements are important for management to make decisions. Financial statements also represent your business to lenders, potential buyers, partners, and other interested parties. We will work closely with you to develop and finalize timely and accurate financial statements.
